Welcome to Laguna College of Business and Arts

LCBA, for 9 decades, has provided quality education to the youth and adults of Calamba City and beyond. Through the
years, it has embraced the changes in the educational system and innovated accordingly. Inspired by the principles of
Dr. Jose P. Rizal, it has transformed many lives and still continues up to the present.

Academic Levels/Stages

Elementary

Foundational learning for young students. Developing essential literacy, numeracy, and basic social skills.

Junior High School

Providing a more advanced curriculum and preparing students for SHS.

Senior High School

covers the final two years of the K to 12 program (Grades 11-12), focusing on specialized, career-ready education.

College

It serves as a bridge between adolescence and adulthood, fostering intellectual growth, personal development, and career preparation.
