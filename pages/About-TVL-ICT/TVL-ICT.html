TVL – Information and Communication Technology

About the Strand

The TVL – ICT Strand (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood – Information and Communications Technology) is designed for students who are passionate about computers and digital technology.


Students gain practical knowledge in programming, computer servicing, web development, and multimedia design. The strand focuses on hands-on learning to prepare students for real-world technology careers.


Graduates of this strand can pursue employment in the IT industry, freelancing opportunities, or continue their studies in college programs related to technology and digital innovation.

Main Goals of TVL – ICT

Technical Skills

Students learn computer hardware, software installation, and troubleshooting.

Real-World Training

Hands-on projects in programming, networking, and digital production.

Job Readiness

Graduates gain skills needed for entry-level IT jobs.

College Preparation

Provides a strong foundation for future IT-related college programs.
